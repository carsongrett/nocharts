About the Company

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The company offers iPhone, Mac, iPad, and wearables, home, and accessories. Apple also sells various related services, including AppleCare, cloud services, and digital content. The company serves consumers, small and mid-sized businesses, and education, enterprise, and government customers through its retail and online stores, and direct sales force, as well as through third-party cellular network carriers, wholesalers, retailers, and resellers.
